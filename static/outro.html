About

I made this tool to demonstrate the power of compounding interest, and to teach myself about reactive web development.

Interesting Cases

Assuming an inflation adjusted return of 7%, around the average return for the stock market, a person who saves 100 per month for 10 years, then saves nothing for 30 years is better off than a person who saves nothing for 10 years, then saves 100 per month for 30 years!

With a lower return of 3.5%, roughly what we can expect on bonds, 14 years of saving beats 26 years of saving.

Technology

The application is written in the Elm programming language, and is available as free software on GitHub.
